The Program

Cyber Inspire offers 3 tracks, each with tailored material to best serve the target audience. Offerings are flexible to meet the need of any organization and can be one-time, on-going, and virtual.

Promote security awareness amongst all ages and nurture a passion for cyber defense amongst the next generation through:

  • Collaboration
  • Innovative Programs
  • Educational Initiatives

Three Tracks

Tailored experiences for every audience.

1

Engage Kids

Fast-paced, engaging activities focused on: Online safety, presence & controls, Scam Awareness, Cybersecurity skills & career options.

2

Empower Girls

Customized offerings aiming to spark girls' interest in Cybersecurity to further efforts for increased women's representation in STEM.

3

Educate Adults

Materials and access to references focused on scam awareness, privacy settings, and general online safety.
